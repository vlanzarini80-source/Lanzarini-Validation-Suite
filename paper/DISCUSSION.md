# Discussion

## Purpose

This document discusses the interpretation of the publicly documented validation results of the Lanzarini Validation Suite.

Its purpose is to clarify which conclusions are supported by the available experimental evidence and to distinguish them from questions that remain outside the scope of the current validation.

---

## Interpretation of the Validation Results

The Validation Suite documents the execution of a structured public validation workflow for the experimentally evaluated configurations.

The reported validation evidence documents the outcome of the corresponding validation activities under the documented experimental conditions.

Interpretation of the reported results should remain within the documented validation scope.

---

## Reproducibility

The repository provides publicly documented validation methodology together with reproducibility artifacts intended to facilitate independent inspection of the reported validation workflow.

Reproducibility depends on reproducing the documented experimental environment and validation procedure.

---

## Scope of the Evidence

The available evidence applies only to the experimentally evaluated configurations documented in this repository.

The reported validation results should not be interpreted as demonstrating universal correctness, universal performance behavior, or applicability across all hardware platforms, software environments, workloads, or model architectures.

---

## Scientific Contribution

This repository documents an evidence-based validation and reproducibility framework for an experimentally evaluated sparse-local attention operator.

Its contribution is the public documentation of the validation methodology, reproducibility workflow, supporting documentation, and experimentally documented validation evidence.

No claim is made regarding the introduction of a novel mathematical attention formulation.

---

## Limitations

Interpretation of the reported validation results is subject to the documented experimental limitations.

Known limitations are described in:

```text
docs/LIMITATIONS.md
```

These limitations should be considered when interpreting the reported validation evidence.

---

## Future Development

Future revisions of the Validation Suite may extend the documented validation evidence through additional experiments, benchmark protocols, hardware platforms, software environments, and reproducibility procedures.

Any future conclusions should remain supported by publicly documented experimental evidence.

---

## Summary

The Lanzarini Validation Suite provides a documented framework for public validation and reproducibility.

The available evidence documents the reported validation activities within the documented experimental scope, while broader conclusions remain outside the scope of the current repository.
