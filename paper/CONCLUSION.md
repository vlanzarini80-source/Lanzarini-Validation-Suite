# Conclusion

## Purpose

This document summarizes the scope and current status of the Lanzarini Validation Suite.

It does not introduce new experimental results or scientific claims.

---

## Summary

The Lanzarini Validation Suite provides a public validation and reproducibility framework for an experimentally evaluated sparse-local attention operator.

The repository documents:

- the mathematical specification of the evaluated operator;
- the public validation methodology;
- correctness documentation;
- computational complexity analysis;
- benchmark scope documentation;
- documented limitations;
- reproducibility artifacts;
- evidence-based scientific claims.

The repository is intended to support transparent documentation, independent inspection, and reproducibility of the documented validation workflow.

---

## Scope of the Contribution

The primary contribution of this repository is the public documentation of a validation and reproducibility framework.

The evaluated operator follows the established sparse-local (sliding-window) causal attention formulation documented in this repository.

No claim is made regarding the introduction of a novel mathematical attention operator or attention mechanism.

The proprietary implementation evaluated by the Validation Suite is intentionally outside the scope of this public repository.

---

## Evidence

Scientific claims are limited to publicly documented experimental evidence.

The reported validation results should be interpreted only within the documented experimental conditions.

Configurations that have not been experimentally evaluated are outside the scope of the reported evidence.

---

## Future Work

Future revisions of the Validation Suite may extend the available validation evidence through additional experiments, hardware platforms, software environments, workloads, benchmark protocols, and reproducibility procedures.

Any future scientific claims should remain supported by publicly documented experimental evidence.

---

## Final Remarks

The Lanzarini Validation Suite is intended to provide a transparent, evidence-based framework for documenting, validating, and reproducing experimentally evaluated sparse-local attention research.

Its primary contribution is the public documentation of the validation process, the associated reproducibility framework, and the experimental evidence reported in this repository.
