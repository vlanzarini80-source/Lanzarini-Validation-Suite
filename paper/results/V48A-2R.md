# V48A-2R: Sparse-Local NVML Energy Repeat-Loop Audit

## Scope

This document reports the preserved experimental results of the V48A-2R energy audit.

Only experimentally measured results are reported.

No estimated, reconstructed or fabricated values are included.

Whenever the original JSON or CSV artifacts are not currently available, only preserved numerical summaries are reported.

---

## Purpose

The objective of this checkpoint was to measure runtime and energy efficiency of the Lanzarini sparse-local attention kernel under a fixed experimental configuration using NVIDIA NVML power sampling.

This checkpoint evaluated a single operating regime.

---

- GPU: NVIDIA A100-SXM4-80GB

## Preserved Performance Summary

For the tested configuration:

- throughput improvement versus SDPA local-mask: approximately **36×**
- energy per token reduction versus SDPA local-mask: approximately **97.8%**

These values refer only to the tested configuration.

---

## Interpretation

Within the evaluated operating regime:

- numerical correctness passed;
- runtime and energy measurements were successfully collected;
- the reported throughput and energy improvements apply only to the tested configuration.

These measurements must not be interpreted as universal performance or energy improvements.

---

## Limitations

The preserved numerical summary does not include:

- average GPU power;
- peak GPU power;
- total energy in joules;
- complete NVML trace.

Those values are therefore intentionally omitted.
