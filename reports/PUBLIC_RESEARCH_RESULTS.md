# Public Research Results

## Purpose

This page collects all experimentally verified research results that are publicly released as part of the Lanzarini Validation Suite.

Only measurements obtained through reproducible experiments are included.

No estimated, simulated, or unpublished results are reported.

---

# Current Public Status

The public repository currently provides:

- validation methodology;
- validation workflow;
- artifact integrity verification;
- reproducibility documentation;
- benchmark policy;
- evidence traceability.

The proprietary sparse-local Triton implementation is intentionally excluded.

---
